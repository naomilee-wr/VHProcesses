=====================================================
Product Architect - Jira Agile Responsibilities
=====================================================

|

**Purpose**
-----------

This document consolidates the various tasks and expectations fulfilled by Product Architect during the Feature/Epic Development Process.

|


**Important Reference**
-----------------------
`Feature Grooming and Workflow <../../../SupplementaryGuidelines/FeatureMgmt/FeatureGrooming_Workflow_SG.html>`__

|

**Responsibilities**
--------------------

- The Product Architect is involved in the **Selected for Development, In Progress and Done** states of a Feature/Epic

  - **Selected for Development**
  
    - Ensure the requirements are understood
    - Dependencies and Impacts are identified
    - Ensures the "PA Owner Field" populated
	
      - Indicates to stakeholders who is responsible for the Epic's architectural oversight
	  
    - Ensures the "PA Tracking Field" is set to SprintReady or NoAction
	
      - Indicates the level of oversight required by the Feature developers
	  
    - Ensures the Feature/Epic Definition is complete
      - Product Architect ultimately must understand and agree to the content of a feature/epic before promoting them to the next state
	  
    - Creates a HLD Story (if needed)
    - Creates a validation story (if needed)
    - Creates a POC Story Created (if needed)
	
  - **In Progress**
    - Scrum Teams must work with the Product Architect to ensure the implementation meets the requirements
		
  - **Done**
    - Ensure the Definition of Done is met
    - "PA Tracking Field" is set to Accepted

Supplementary Guidelines¶

The Supplementary Guidance supports the process standards and guidelines and provides further guidance to the team. The guidelines are either created by Engineering or support teams outside of Engineering (e.g., Tools, Product Management, PMO, or CSO) and maintained on the Team Collaboration system (e.g., Jive, SharePoint).

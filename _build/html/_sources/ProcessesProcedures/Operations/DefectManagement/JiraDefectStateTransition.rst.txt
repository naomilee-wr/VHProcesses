================================
Jira Defect State Transition
================================

|

The Jira defect tracking tool is located at: https://jira.wrs.com.

Jira has seven defect states, as shown in the diagram below.

|image0| 

When a defect is in the Resolved state, a resolution is chosen: Fixed, Won’t Fix, Duplicate, Cannot Reproduce, Rejected.

When a defect is placed in On Hold, a reason must be specified: None, Backlog, Waiting for Reporter, Waiting for Vendor.
